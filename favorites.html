My Favorite Hobbies and Interests

I have a lot of things that I like to do in my free time! I'll share some of them here!!!!!

SAXOPHONE

I love playing the alto saxophone! I've played it since 6th grade, and I still play it today. I always like instruments that work well as solo-instruments, and I think the alto saxophone fits that very well! My skill level is far from advanced on the saxophone, but I'm still able to sight-read popular songs if I ever feel like it!

ONE PIECE

I love One Piece. I started it last summer. I reached episode 1000 in just 6 months after last summer ended. I really like the unique story that Eiichiro Oda created for One Piece, and I definitely love Luffy's rubber power. One Piece was what made physics class so much fun for me, because I could keep on comparing Luffy's attacks with the workings of physics!!!

Luffy drank too much water

Martial Arts

My form is not good and my balance is very off. However, I practice punching and kicking for fun, and use it more as a form of exercise rather than a formal skill. I'm the best martial artist in my dojo! (my dojo is the backyard and I am the only person in the backyard) Bruce Lee's kicking is my end goal, but I know I won't exactly reach that point. Self-learning martial arts is my 100% favorite exercise, even if I'm not good at it!!!!!

ROBOTICS

I'm trying to build a pill counter using a Raspberry Pi. I've seen pill counters cost around $8,000 along with over $1,000 in yearly maitenance. I wanted to try to build a prototype for a cost-effective model, and I settled on a rotating disc attatched to a motor! So far I have yet to complete it, but I believe it will be 100% done by next month!
