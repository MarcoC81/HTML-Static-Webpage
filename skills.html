1: Know Your Audience

This is 100% the most important part! If you don't know your audience, you won't know how to appeal to them! If you want to make a college acceptance reactions video and use it to get popular, then your audience will be mostly high school freshmen to seniors! You must alter your sense of humor accordingly, and not sound pretentious! There are many factors to consider, but you must at least understand who your audience is and how they think! For the sake of clarity, I will be creating a college acceptance reactions video foundation for this website!

2: Create a Captivating Thumbnail

The thumbnail is the first thing the consumer will see! You absolutely have to make it captivating, making use of what your audience is interested in! If you need a good thumbnail creator, you can use Canva! It's free, and has a lot of features! Here are some pointers for making a good thumbnail:

  • Try not let your thumbnail's text be COMPLETELY identical to the video title! Change it up by 1 or 2 words!
  • Show open-sourced images relating to your video's topic! Make sure they are relevant and easy-to-read!
  • Make your thumbnail simple. Anyone that scrolls by should be able to understand it!
  • Clickbait is okay, but don't do it to the point of it becoming unethical! You want to get views, but you don't want your viewer to leave the video within the first second! That will only hurt your video's chances of success!

3: Play the Right Music

Good background music is integral for increasing your watch time! You must know your audience to know what music to put in the background. If you were a Roblox youtuber, you could consider putting non-copyright EDM songs. For college reaction videos, you would usually want to use lofi or calm music, to lower the heartrate of your consumer. I've attatched an example of a lofi song. 4: Analyze Your Statistics!

Statistics are extremely important! If you're making your first video, you don't have to worry about this. However, if you have 1 or more videos uploaded, you can check out your analytics! By analyzing these, you can see how people found you, and get a good idea of the type of audience you have!
