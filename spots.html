My Most and Least Favorite Things in NYC

NYC can be a very cool place! There are some nice areas, and some areas that I'm not a huge fan of! Let's take a look around!

ICHIRAN

ICHIRAN is a ramen shop that lets you customize your own ramen to its individual ingredients! It's a very cool restaurant that gives you private seating if you are eating alone, or you can sit in the public seats. The quality of food there is 10/10, though the drawback is that it is pretty expensive! There are many locations that can be found through a quick google search. Enter at your own risk. D:

ICHIRAN has a very unique bathroom!

Check out Ichiran's USA website!

SUNSET PARK

Sunset Park is massive! Wherever you go, you'll find yourself surrounded by trees or around sports equipment that you can play with! What's amazing is that they have an entire recreational center there too, with its pool open almost every day!

The pool is massive! Even when it's crowded there's still space to swim laps!
